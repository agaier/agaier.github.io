Short Bio

I'm a nomadic American researcher with a research background spanning evolutionary computation, robotics, and machine learning. I mostly work in and around biologically-inspired computation with neuroevolution, quality-diversity, and novelty-based approaches.

My doctoral work centers on the integration of evolutionary and ML techniques -- with particular application to real-world design and control problems -- but am fascinated by questions of creativity, embodiment, and innateness in machine intelligence.
